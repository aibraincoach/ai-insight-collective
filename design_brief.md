# design_brief.md — For Claude Design

## What you're designing
1. The marketing site (5 pages + legal) for Insight Dashboard by AI Insight Collective.
2. The /demo dashboard page — a static page styled as the real product UI. This is the most important deliverable: three marketing screenshots come from it and it gets shown live on sales calls.

## Audience
Business owners, BIA executives, nonprofit directors, association leads. Not developers. They should feel "this company is sharp and this product tells me things I didn't know about my own business."

## Tone
Confident, clean, executive. Closer to a modern fintech/analytics product (think Stripe, Linear, Mercury territory) than to a local web agency. It must NOT look like a template — this site's entire job is credibility.

## Hard constraints
- Vanilla HTML/CSS only. No frameworks, no Tailwind, no build step. One stylesheet.
- System font stack or one self-hosted font max. No external font CDNs if avoidable.
- Must hold up at 375px and 1440px.
- Dark-on-light for marketing pages. The /demo dashboard may go dark UI if it photographs better — your call.

## Required deliverables back to PM
1. Design tokens: color palette, type scale, spacing scale, as CSS custom properties ready to paste into css/site.css.
2. Layout direction per page (low-fi is fine).
3. Full visual design for /demo: executive KPI row, traffic sources chart, AI insights panel (judgment statements, not raw stats), weekly briefing card. Fictional client, plausible numbers.
4. og-image concept.

## Copy anchors (locked, design around these)
- Hero: "We show you where the money comes from — and where you're leaving it on the table."
- Product name treatment: Insight Dashboard, by AI Insight Collective.
- CTA everywhere: "Book a Demo."

## What to avoid
- Stock photos of handshakes, generic "AI" brain graphics, purple-gradient AI clichés.
- Anything that reads agency or consulting.
- Canadian references of any kind.
